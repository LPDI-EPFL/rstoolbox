rstoolbox.utils.mutations¶

rstoolbox.utils.mutations(seqID='A')¶

RosettaScript to execute a RESFILE.

Parameters:seqID (str) – Identifier of the sequence of interest
Returns:str

See also

DesignFrame.apply_resfile()

Example

In [1]: from rstoolbox.utils import mutations
   ...: print(mutations())
   ...: 
<ROSETTASCRIPTS>
    <TASKOPERATIONS>
        <ReadResfile name="targets" filename="%%resfile%%"/>
    </TASKOPERATIONS>
    <MOVERS>
        <PackRotamersMover name="packrot" task_operations="targets" />
        <AddJobPairData name="annotate" value_type="string"
                        key="resfile_A" value="%%resfile%%" />
    </MOVERS>
    <PROTOCOLS>
        <Add mover="packrot" />
        <Add mover="annotate" />
    </PROTOCOLS>
</ROSETTASCRIPTS>
